The sourcing process

From the initial idea to delivery — we accompany you through every step of the process.

STEP 1

Needs analysis & strategy discussion

It all starts with your vision. We use a structured questionnaire to capture your exact requirements. Together, we assess feasibility and lay the foundation for smooth production.

STEP 2

Manufacturer selection & price negotiation

Our teams in Germany and China filter through thousands of manufacturers to find the best partners for you. We rigorously compare offers and negotiate hard on your behalf to secure the best margins.

STEP 3

Market conformity & certification

We won't leave you to navigate the legal maze alone. Our team will review all relevant EU directives and labeling requirements for your product. You'll receive a precise roadmap and all the necessary certificates for a legally compliant sales launch in Germany.

STEP 4

Quality check on the product sample

See for yourself the quality. You'll receive a physical product sample for final inspection of feel, workmanship, and function. Only when you are 100% satisfied and give your approval will we start production.

STEP 5

Production & Quality Inspection

We leave nothing to chance. During production, our experts conduct rigorous quality controls on-site at the factories. Any deviation is immediately documented and corrected, ensuring you receive precisely the quality we specified in the sample.

STEP 6

Logistics, Customs & Full-Service Import

Sit back and relax while we handle the complex logistics. From secure shipping and customs clearance to warehousing or direct delivery — we get your goods to their destination efficiently and on time.

FAQ

How do you prevent me from ending up with bad factories?
We don't rely on online profiles. We verify business licenses, certificates, and — when necessary — commission audits directly on-site. Years of experience let us recognize warning signs in suppliers before a single euro changes hands.
Do you also handle the price negotiations?
Absolutely. It's one of the biggest levers for your margin. We know local market pricing and negotiate eye-to-eye in the native language. We often secure conditions a foreign buyer could never reach through platforms like Alibaba.
Are you looking for suppliers only in China or worldwide?
Our focus and deepest expertise — through our network and on-the-ground teams — sits in China. Depending on product category and requirement, we also evaluate capacity in other markets so you always get the best price-quality ratio.
What happens if the quality of the samples does not meet my expectations?
That's exactly where our work begins. We analyze the weak points, communicate every revision directly to the manufacturer, and stay on the case until the sample matches your specifications precisely. Only when you are 100% satisfied do we release production.
